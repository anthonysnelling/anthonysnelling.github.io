Hi! I am Anthony Snelling and this is a website that I have created to show off some of my art and coding projects. I am a graduate from Eastern Michigan University with a degree in Computer Science, I also studied at Washtenaw Community College with a degree in 3D animation and also studied character animation at Animation Mentor.

I have a strong interest in mobile app and web development as well as computer graphics and game development. I love learning new technologies and new things in general, I am always trying to improve and learn new things. I feel like I have a thousand projects I would like to work on, but not nearly enough time.

If I am not working on something I am probably playing games, playing guitar, watching a movie, daydreaming, or trying to learn something new.
